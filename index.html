Guide

Answering Trivia: Correct answers yield XP, Gold, and hit standard enemies or Bosses. Timer timeout or incorrect replies damage your HP.

Party Companions: Build a cooperative alliance. You can recruit distinct companions. They provide permanent passive benefits during fights.

Casting Spells: Use MP in battle to freeze times, mend health, or use Clairvoyance.

Boss Fights: Encounters trigger on every 4th non-safe-caravan floor. Land consecutive strikes to win!
